ABOUT US

Carnegie Architectural Stone LLC is New York City’s premier Limestone, Cast Stone and exterior granite fabricator.

Founded in 1981 in upstate New York initially as a granite monument fabricators over years Carnegie joint ventured with NYC based cast stone manufacturer Stone Age. Today we are run by 2nd and 3rd generation of stone crafters with decades of industry experience and through continued capital improvements and emphasis on quality Carnegie Architectural Stone has emerged as NYC’s leader in Indiana Limestone Fabrication.

Carnegie Architectural Stone LLC specializes in custom fabrication of Indiana Limestone, cast stone and granite for new construction and restoration projects.

For more information visit us on contact page or gallery or in one our 2 locations: Offices and Showroom in Sunset Park, Brooklyn or manufacturing facility in Westtown NY.
